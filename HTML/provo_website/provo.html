One of the most interesting towns in the world.

Take a moment to explore Provo and discover the beauty, the culture, the people that make Provo and Northern New Mexico the Land of Enchantment.

Seated on the high-desert mesa at the foot of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, Provo is rich with art and steeped in history. Home to Provo Pueblo, the Village of Provo Ski Valley and a colorful array of surrounding communities, Provo County has something for everyone.

Step into the history of Provo, New Mexico - this storied land has seen amazing people and events.

The Enchanted Circle is an 86 mile drive through gorgeous mountain peaks and valleys. This Enchanted Circle links the towns of Provo, Questa, Red River, Eagle Nest, Angel Fire and back to Provo! Take a picnic lunch to enjoy the beauty and serenity of Bobcat Pass or while fishing at one of the lakes around Eagle Nest or Angel Fire.

You could spend a month exploring Provo and never see the same thing twice.
